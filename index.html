Privacy Policy — Ink Dynasty

Effective date: 2026-06-29 · App: Ink Dynasty (com.kaomidevstudio.inkdynasty) · Developer: Kaomi Dev Studio · Contact: kaomiwings@gmail.com

Who this app is for

Ink Dynasty is an educational game that teaches Mandarin Chinese to children (ages 8–14) through a Three Kingdoms adventure. We have designed it to comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families policy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

What we DO NOT collect

  • We do not ask for or store your real name. Each player is given an auto-generated nickname (for example, "General Tiger 7421"). You never type a name.
  • We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, precise location, or advertising identifiers.
  • We do not show third-party advertising.
  • We do not record, store, or transmit your voice. (See "Microphone" below.)
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Microphone

The app asks for microphone permission so you can practice pronouncing Chinese words out loud. All speech recognition happens on your device. The audio is processed locally by an on-device model and is never uploaded, stored, or sent to us or anyone else. If you decline microphone permission, you can still play; only the speaking-practice features are unavailable.

Optional online features

Some optional features send a small amount of non-personal data to our backend service (Supabase). None of it identifies you personally, and playing offline sends nothing:

  • Leaderboards: your auto-generated nickname (e.g. "General Tiger 7421"), your score, the game mode, and a random one-time code used only to avoid duplicate entries.
  • Kingdom War / faction events: your chosen kingdom and the experience points you contributed that week.
  • Friend challenges: randomly generated friend codes, the practice phrase, and a score — no names.
  • Classes: if you join or create a class, the class code and your auto-generated nickname.

We do not offer account sign-in, so we never collect a Google account, email address, or real name.

Downloaded game content

To keep the initial download small, voice lines and cutscene videos are downloaded from our content delivery service (Supabase Storage) the first time you play. These requests transfer game files only; they do not send personal information about you.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional one-time purchases to unlock additional story content. Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details. A parental gate (a math question) protects the purchase screen so a child cannot buy content without an adult. Purchases are governed by Google Play's own terms and privacy policy.

Notifications

With your permission, the app may send local reminders to continue learning. These are generated on your device. We do not send remote/push notifications and do not collect data to target notifications.

Data retention and deletion

Because we do not collect personal information, there is no personal account to delete. Leaderboard entries contain only a nickname and a score. To request removal of a leaderboard entry, email us at the contact address above.

Children's privacy (COPPA / Families)

This app is directed to children. Consistent with COPPA, we do not collect personal information from children. The only data ever sent off the device is the non-personal data described above, and only when a player opts to use those online features.

Changes to this policy

If we change our data practices we will update this page and the effective date above.
